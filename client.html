    About Us

    Aligned Rebar is a dedicated team of rebar professionals, established in 2023 with the goal of delivering precise, efficient, and reliable reinforcement solutions to the construction industry.

    With a growing team of 25 experienced engineers, we specialize in two core services:

    • Rebar Estimation
    • Rebar Detailing

    Our mission is to support contractors, fabricators, and builders with accurate rebar drawings and material take-offs that help reduce project costs, eliminate errors, and streamline site execution.

    We take pride in combining technical expertise with fast turnaround times, ensuring our clients receive top-tier service on every project—whether it's residential, commercial, or infrastructure work.

    Let us help you build with confidence.

    Our Projects

    Reben Retailing

    We offer comprehensive rebar detailing services tailored to meet international standards and project-specific requirements. Our team delivers both 2D and 3D rebar shop drawings, complete with accurate Bar Bending Schedules (BBS) to support smooth fabrication and on-site installation.

    Reben Estimation

    Industry-leading accuracy in rebar estimation with competitive per-ton pricing. All estimates strictly follow CRSI Standard Practice guidelines. Compliance with contract documents and local building codes ensured. Confidential estimates exclusively for submitting contractors/fabricators.

    Reben Retailing

    Our detailing services include

    • 2D Rebar Shop Drawings with clear placement instructions, lap details, and section views.
    • 3D Rebar Modeling for enhanced visualization, clash detection, and coordination with other trades.
    • Bar Bending Schedules (BBS) optimized for cutting and bending, in standard formats.
    • Reinforcement Drawings for all structural elements including slabs, beams, columns, walls, footings, and more.

    Code Compliance

    We follow international standards based on project location

    • U.S. – ACI 318, CRSI (Concrete Reinforcing Steel Institute) standards
    • U.K. – BS 8666, BS 8110, and Eurocode 2 (EN 1992)
    • U.A.E. – British Standards (BS) and ACI codes, based on project specifications

    Reben Estimation

    Our detailing services include

    • All estimates will conform to the CRSI Code of Standard Practice..
    • For estimating reinforcing materials, when not in conflict with the contract documents, local or state building codes apply.
    • Estimates done by Arrow Services remain confidential.strThey will only be available to the contractor or fabricator who supplies the document for estimating.
    • Estimates are done in aSa Program.
